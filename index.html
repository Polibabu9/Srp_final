How the Dome Digester Works

The dome digester processes organic waste to produce biogas and compost, providing renewable energy and reducing landfill waste.

  • Waste Collection
  • Anaerobic Digestion
  • Biogas Production
  • Compost Output

Benefits

Reduces Landfill Waste

Decreases the amount of waste sent to landfills by converting it to energy and fertilizer.

Produces Renewable Energy

Generates biogas which can be used as a fuel, reducing reliance on non-renewable resources.

Improves Soil Quality

Produces compost, a natural fertilizer that enriches soil and promotes plant growth.
